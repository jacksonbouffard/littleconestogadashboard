Little Conestoga Dashboard

Welcome to the Little Conestoga Watershed Dashboard. Created by the Penn State Agriculture and Environment Center. This dataset of Best Management Practices was primarily created using the AEC's 2023 Watershed Assessment, utilitizing LandStudies' 2015 Watershed Action Plan, with contributions from several local partners.

Parcel-level projects represent only potential projects that have been merged based off of landownwership. These projects have been prioritized based on a multifactor scoring system which considers: BMP types, priority subwatersheds, and catchment IBI scores. More information can be found here: Parcel-Level Prioritization Methodology. BMP survey points show all BMPs; potential, implemented, planned, and are placed at the exact BMP location.

Use the filters on the left to explore different categories of projects and priority areas.
